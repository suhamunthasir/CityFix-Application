Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about CityFix

Reporting Issues

What types of problems can I report on CityFix?

You can report various issues that fall under the responsibility of your local council, including:

  • Roads & Pavements: Potholes, damaged roads, broken pavements
  • Street Lighting: Broken or faulty street lights
  • Waste Management: Missed garbage collections, overflowing bins
  • Parks & Public Spaces: Damaged playground equipment, overgrown vegetation
  • Drainage & Flooding: Blocked drains, flooding issues

If you're unsure whether a problem is appropriate for CityFix, please report it anyway - we'll make sure it reaches the right department.

How precise does my location need to be when reporting?

The more precise your location, the easier it is for council workers to find and fix the problem. Here are some tips for accurate location reporting:

  • Use the map to pinpoint the exact location of the problem
  • Add descriptive details in the location field (e.g., "outside house number 25," "near the bus stop")
  • If possible, include a photo that shows landmarks to help identify the location

Don't worry if you can't be extremely precise - council workers are familiar with their areas and can usually find problems based on general descriptions.

Should I include photos with my report?

Yes, photos are extremely helpful for several reasons:

  • They help council workers identify the exact problem
  • They provide evidence of the issue's severity
  • They can help prioritize urgent problems
  • They make it easier to verify when a problem has been fixed

When taking photos:

  • Try to capture the problem clearly
  • Include some context (e.g., nearby landmarks)
  • Take photos from different angles if helpful
  • Ensure the photo is in focus and well-lit

Photos are optional but strongly recommended for better results.

Tracking Reports

How can I track the status of my reports?

You can track your reports in several ways:

  1. My Reports Dashboard: Log in to your account and visit the "My Reports" section to see all your submissions and their current status.
  2. Email Updates: We'll send you email notifications when there are updates on your reports.
  3. Report Page: Each report has its own page where you can see updates and comments from the council.

Reports typically go through these stages: Submitted ‚Üí Acknowledged ‚Üí In Progress ‚Üí Fixed ‚Üí Closed.

How long does it take for problems to get fixed?

The time it takes to fix a problem varies depending on several factors:

  • Type of problem: Some issues (like dangerous potholes) are prioritized over others
  • Council resources: Different councils have different response times
  • Severity: More serious problems tend to get faster responses
  • Weather conditions: Some repairs depend on suitable weather

Most councils aim to acknowledge reports within 3-5 working days. You can check typical response times for your local council on their website.

What should I do if my report hasn't been addressed?

If your report seems to be taking longer than expected, here are some steps you can take:

  1. Check for updates: Log in to your account and check if there are any updates on your report page.
  2. Add more information: If you have additional details or photos, add them to your report to help the council.
  3. Contact the council directly: Use the contact information on the report page to follow up with the relevant department.
  4. Escalate if necessary: If the problem is dangerous and hasn't been addressed, you can contact your local councillor.

Account Help

Do I need an account to report problems?

Yes, you need to create a CityFix account to report problems. Having an account offers several benefits:

  • Track the status of your reports
  • Receive email updates when there's progress on your reports
  • Save your location preferences
  • Easily submit multiple reports
  • Manage your notification settings

Creating an account is quick, free, and requires only an email address.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, you can reset it easily:

  1. Go to the Sign In page
  2. Click on "Forgot your password?"
  3. Enter the email address associated with your account
  4. Check your email for a password reset link
  5. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password

If you don't receive the password reset email within a few minutes, check your spam folder. If you still can't find it, please contact our support team.

Can I change my email address or personal details?

Yes, you can update your account information at any time:

  1. Sign in to your CityFix account
  2. Click on your profile name in the top right corner
  3. Select "Account Settings" from the dropdown menu
  4. Update your email address, name, or other details
  5. Click "Save Changes" to confirm

If you change your email address, we'll send a confirmation email to the new address to verify it.
